Fajitas

fried chicken with peppers and onions wrapped in tortilla bread

ingrediants

  • tortilla bread
  • chicken breast
  • peppers
  • onions
  • spices

steps

  1. cut the onions, peppers and chicken.
  2. start frying the onions and peppers
  3. after approximately five minutes add the chicken to the frying pan
  4. add your spices
  5. cook until it's ready
  6. wrap the contents of the frying pan in your tortilla bread and enjoy
